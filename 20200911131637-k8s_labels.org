#+title: k8s labels

- Labels can be added to any K8s object, including nodes.
- They are used to organize these objects, and targeting a subset of your resources for whatever yr needs:
    - finding pods in kubectl
    - deleting or patching en masse
    - a method for searching or filtering with kubectl
